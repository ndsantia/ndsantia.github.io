DUNE Collaboration Research

Working with Dr. Jesse Thaler and Dr. Jesse Micallef at MIT's Institute for Artificial Intelligence and Fundamental Interactions, I worked on analysis software for DUNE's near detector. I retooled a machine learning architecture of graph neural networks designed for liquid argon time projection chambers to work with solid scintillator detectors.

MINERvA Experiment Research

Working with Dr. McFarland and Joel Elias at the University of Rochester, I conducted an analysis on multi-nucleon knockout events in the MINERvA experiment.

Myxobacterial Modeling Research

Working with Dr. Joseph Hibdon and Dr. Emina Stojkovic, I derived a set of limit conditions based on laboratory results that could accurately model unconventional bacterial growth.
