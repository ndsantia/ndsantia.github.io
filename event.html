νμ + n → μ− + p

The central four-module volume represents the 2×2 liquid-argon time projection chamber at the DUNE near detector. The surrounding two boxes are MINERvA solid scintillator detectors. This is a toy event, although it looks pretty close to the real thing. I built machine learning tools that allowed us to make important predictions about events like this one.
